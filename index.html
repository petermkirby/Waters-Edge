About Water's Edge Plumbing & Heating

Owner-operated, community-rooted, and proud to serve the South Shore for over 25 years.

Water's Edge Plumbing & Heating was founded by Jon Harght on a simple belief: every homeowner deserves an honest plumber they can trust. Owner-operated since our founding, you can count on Jon to personally service your home or business with the care it deserves.

Locally owned and rooted in Marshfield, we've been serving Plymouth and Norfolk County's residential and commercial plumbing needs since 2013. Our reputation for honesty, reliability, and quality work at competitive prices is something we guard fiercely — it's why our customers keep calling us back and referring their neighbors.

Our team of licensed plumbing professionals is ready to tackle anything from your smallest repair to your largest project. We service the South Shore from Scituate to Plymouth and everywhere in between. Our workmanship meets the highest Massachusetts standards, and we use the brand-name products you can trust — from practical to premium lines.

Our Services

From routine maintenance to full system installations, Water's Edge handles every job with care and expertise.

01

Water Heater Repair & Installation

There are many water heater options available to homeowners today — tank, tankless, heat pump, and solar. Water's Edge Plumbing & Heating can help you decide which model will best meet your family's needs, then install it correctly and efficiently. We service all major brands.

02

Boiler Repair & Installation

From emergency heating system repairs — including radiators and boilers — to new furnace installation, call Water's Edge for fast and professional service to ensure your comfort. We understand how critical heat is during New England winters.

03

Bathroom Renovation

When you're ready to give your bathroom an update, call Water's Edge Plumbing & Heating. We handle all the plumbing for complete renovations — from rough-in plumbing for new layouts to fixture installation that gives your bath a spa-like feel.

04

Kitchen Renovation

Open-concept living often requires renovating the kitchen — moving plumbing and appliances to better meet your family's needs. We coordinate with your contractor to make sure every pipe, drain, and gas line is exactly where it needs to be.

05

Water & Gas Meters

Building a new house or upgrading your system? Call Water's Edge to install new gas and water meters and run your water and gas pipes to code. We work closely with municipal inspectors to ensure a smooth approval process.

06

Solar Water Heaters & Pool Heaters

Solar hot water systems are environmentally friendly and can be installed to blend seamlessly with your home's architecture. We also install and service pool heaters to extend your swimming season here on the South Shore.

07

Troubleshooting Leaks & Clogs

Got leaks or clogs in sinks, toilets, faucets, bathtubs, or sewer pipes? Call Water's Edge to keep everything running smoothly. We use the latest diagnostic tools to find the problem fast and fix it right.

08

Repair / Replace / Install Plumbing Fixtures

If all you need is a new fixture, we do that too. From faucets to tubs, sinks to toilets — we'll get you exactly what you need, properly installed. We work with all brands from budget-friendly to designer lines.

09

Water Piping Repair & Installation

Older homes on the South Shore often have aging pipe systems that need attention. Whether it's a pinhole leak or a full repipe, our team handles copper, PVC, PEX, and other pipe systems with precision.

10

24/7 Emergency Service

Our expert emergency maintenance team is available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, 365 days a year to attend to your problems promptly and competently. Because plumbing emergencies don't wait for business hours.
